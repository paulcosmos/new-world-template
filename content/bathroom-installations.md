### How we Work
To ensure our customer requirements and expectations are always met, we plan and design all of our bathroom installation projects closely with our customers, prior to the installation process. To do this, we call out to the property to assess the current condition of the bathroom as well as take any necessary measurements. Following this, we discuss a range of bathroom specifications including facilities, layout, fixtures, fittings and so much more, with which we produce a bespoke bathroom installation plan and design. This way, we are able to gain an in-depth understanding of our customer's desired results, which in turn allows us to meet and even exceed these expectations once the installation project is complete. 

During our installations, we ensure all health and safety regulations are adhered to, alongside ensuring all fixtures and fittings comply with current safety standards once the installation is complete. This means, when undergoing your bathroom installation you can settle knowing that your property is in safe, trustworthy hands and you can put your confidence in us that all aspects within your bathroom will be installed and working in an optimally safe manner once we have finished the project.
