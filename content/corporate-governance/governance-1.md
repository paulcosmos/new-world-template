## Corporate Governance 
A big part of sustainable corporate governance would aim to work close to government compliance that sound building regulation and reduction of CO2 in our carbon feet print.  

Our Corporate governance would comply with all levels of government, the local community directive and policies that is accountable to all various stakeholders and shareholders.

### Health & Safety & Internal Goals 
{{<company>}} has an Occupational Health and Safety committee that continuously monitors and develops the safety and health of the employees. 

* To Improve the wellbeing of our employees. 
* Zero occupational health incidents, accidents or “near misses”. 
* Monitor the wellbeing of employees and work safety through internal surveys and occupational healthcare reports. 
* Continuously evaluate the best ways to support the wellbeing of our employees and referee our employees to external health care services that we work with.

### Human Rights &amp; Modern Slavery
{{<company>}} respects and supports the protection of internationally proclaimed human rights. We will not tolerate any violations of human rights within the organisation or among our suppliers, partners, customers, or other stakeholders. 

{{<company>}} shall not be in a partnership or in a business relationship with partners or suppliers that breach legislation regarding human rights, modern slavery or child labour. 

Use our guided code of policy to assess all major suppliers and the associated risks with working with them.
