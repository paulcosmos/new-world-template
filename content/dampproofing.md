### Different kinds of damp

Many houses in the UK suffer from damp at some point in time. There are 3 major types of damp. Penetrating damp is caused by water infiltrating through poorly sealed windows, roofs or gutters. Rising damp is caused by lack of (or flaws in) a damp-proof course around the base of the building. Condensation is often found in bathrooms or kitchens and is caused by excess moisture in the air.

Any of these can be a cause for concern. Left untreated, they will damage not only the appearance of your home but also its underlying structure.
