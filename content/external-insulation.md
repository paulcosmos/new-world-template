External insulation is a thermal layer designed to coat the external walls of a property to prevent heat loss through the walls and to create a protective, durable barrier. Additionally, the thermal insulative layers increase wall durability, making the external walls durable. Therefore, not only does external insulation assist with temperature control within a property, but it also makes the walls of the property more resilient.
