# What are **fire doors**, and why are they important?

Fire doors, also known as fire-rated doors, play a crucial role in ensuring safety in buildings. These specialized doors are designed to withstand the spread of fire and smoke, providing valuable time for occupants to evacuate and minimizing property damage.
