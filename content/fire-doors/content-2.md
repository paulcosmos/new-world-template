## Fire-doors **must** be installed properly.

Never attempt to install fire doors yourself as a DIY project, or trust a general joiner to install them. Poorly and incorrectly installed fire doors and frames are a danger to life. It's as simple as that. Always ensure they are installed by a qualified and accredited company, such as us.

If you've had doors fitted recently and you want to ensure they were installed correctly and are compliant, speak to us to arrange [an inspection](/fire-door-inspection.html). We can identify and resolve any issues on the spot.
