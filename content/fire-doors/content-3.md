## Have your fire doors **inspected** by experts regularly.

Fire doors serve as critical components in any building's safety infrastructure. Their ability to resist flames and smoke allows occupants precious time for evacuation while minimizing property damage. To ensure their effectiveness, it is crucial that fire doors are properly installed and regularly maintained according to local regulations and industry standards. 

Regular inspections should be conducted to check for any signs of damage or wear that may compromise their integrity. How often they are inspected depends on many factors, including age of the doors and how often they are in use, but as a baseline it is recommended they should be inspected every 6 months by a qualified inspector.
