## We don't dump it, **we recycle!**

We hate to see perfectly good items wasted. Our aim is to recycle and re-home as many of the items removed as possible, saving them from landfill. 

We find many strange and unusual items, and do our best to give them a new lease of life. 

Be sure to follow us on <a href="https://facebook.com/profile.php?id=100057394512037" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Facebook</a> and <a href="https://instagram.com/kennethouseclearance" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Instagram</a> to stay updated on newly available items for sale.
