## Forget the spray cans. Drain Tags are **made to last** and environmentally friendly.

Ditch the tired approach of spraying markings on the ground. Made from UV stable polyethylene and available in 4 vibrant colours, Drain Tags are long-lasting, weather resistant and quick to install. 

Embossed lettering ensures the codes **will never rub off** or be washed away by rain and can even withstand power washes.

Should you need to dispose of them, they are also **100% recyclable**.
