## Supply Chain 
Our supply chain plays a fundamental part in our success. We like to develop long-term relationships and are committed to working together to ensure we're integrated and flexible with our operations. We recognise the need to actively have support and invest in our supply chain, including promoting innovative operations as well as more efficient ways of working.

## Apprentices
We would proactively offer young Londoners an opportunity into the construction industry through a robust apprentice scheme. Young people are not being made aware of the benefits of getting into work via this route, and are instead being encouraged into further academic studies..

Our apprenticeship programme would promote construction as a good career choice. Apprenticeships offer a greater chance for young people leave school and enter directly into the world of work, learning a trade and earning an income whilst building a career. They also learn about working in a business setting which includes everything from people-skills, answering the phone to talking in meetings and representing the company. This is often missed when young people move on to further education.

We are committed to lifting young people from the streets into construction sites. 

## Our Partnership
Our partnership with local government bodies, with our investment to transform pockets of the neighbourhood that require redevelopment and investment, to help increase better living standards for all residents. 

We also supporting women wishing to work in the construction industry, Women into Construction works with housebuilders and contractors to recruit highly motivated, trained women, helping to reduce skills gaps and create a more gender-equal work-force
