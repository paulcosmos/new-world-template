## Anthony Wolex 
### Founder & CEO
Anthony is the founder and CEO of Chesterfield Homes, a visionary entrepreneur with over two decades of experience in the London property development scene. He acquired his first property at the age of 18, with the strategy of creating added value.

Anthony has a proven track record as a property developer and trader, involving more than £40 million in property transactions. His eye for an opportunity has seen him involved in key strategy regeneration sites, and land assembly opportunities.

His highlights and milestones from 1998 until today include building a real estate business with start-up savings of £3,000 whilst attending college at the age of 18.

Anthony is a self-taught real estate/developer entrepreneur who started with on funding backing, building assets of over £20 million, which included 220 eco-homes and 172 hotels in Barbados.

He has developed a core interest in all aspects of property development, site acquisition, strategic land assembly, urban regeneration and planning gain. He is ambitious and passionate to succeed in business, and this encouraged him to study professional business strategy at the University of West London.
