## Neil Connolly 
### Operations & Project Director

Neil is a senior professional who has operated at strategic and board levels  across several sectors. Having worked in Europe, the Middle East and Australia, Neil has acquired sensitivity to both corporate and cultural differences and thereby developed an aptitude for managing multiple stakeholder arrangements. This includes directing and restructuring businesses and agreements despite the vagaries of international and domestic economic change, whilst maintaining diverse stakeholder interests.

He Plays the role of technical Director, and latterly Director for Urban Regeneration with a total projects GVD of over £1.2bn of regeneration and development projects were secured.

Neil has established strategic partnerships with organisations offering a range of bespoke solutions for private clients in the UK, Europe, China, Balkan states and the Middle East. This has included work in infrastructure, urban regeneration, private residential, hospitality, heritage, leisure and mixed-use schemes.
