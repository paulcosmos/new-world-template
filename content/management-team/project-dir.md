## Mohammed Lashean
### Project Manager

Mohamed is a skilled structural engineer with a focus on cost saving to meet the project budget, in addition to further value engineering to reduce design and construction costs and maximize the profits for real estate developers. He has a master’s degree in structural engineering and is a graduate member of the Institution of Structural Engineers.

Mohammed has more than 20 years of experience in handling commercial/residential/retail building structures on tight budgets. He is interested in earthquake engineering, structural dynamics, the lateral stability analysis of tall buildings, computer modelling, wind/earthquake-resistant design, concrete/steel structures, with a focus on concrete structures. He is experienced in designing projects from conception to completion and has worked in Egypt, the Gulf region, the Middle East and the UK.

Having qualified in Egypt with a Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering, he also gained his master’s on the structural engineering stability of space structures supported with cables. (Minufiya University 1996–2000 Bachelor of Civil Engineering Obtained Fifth Class Minufiya University Membership and Awarding Graduate member, Institution of Structural Engineers).
