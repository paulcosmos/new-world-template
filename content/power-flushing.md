### What Is Power Flushing?

Power flushing is the process of cleaning and restoring the pipes throughout the central heating system. Unfortunately, over time, sludge and debris can accumulate within the heating system which can lead to blockages, resulting in reduced energy efficiency. 

Therefore, the power flushing method of cleansing the internal pipes at high pressure aims to dislodge and remove build-up, allowing the system to function at its highest potential again.

### Benefits of power flushing include:

**Enhanced System Performance:** Power flushing can improve the circulation of hot water throughout your radiators, resulting in even heating and faster warm-up times. This not only makes the property more comfortable, but it also reduces energy consumption and costs. 


**Increased System Lifespan:** By removing harmful deposits and corrosion, power flushing helps to extend the longevity of your central heating system. This can therefore be highly cost-effective through the elimination of replacement costs at a later date.

**Preventative Maintenance:** Power flushing is not only a solution for existing problems but also a preventative measure to avoid future issues. Regular maintenance is a perfect way of keeping the system running smoothly, year after year.
