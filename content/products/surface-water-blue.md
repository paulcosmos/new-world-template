## Blue Surface Water (SW) Drain Tags

Surface water is simply rainwater that collects on a property and escapes to nearby drains to collect in the sewer system. This water can drain off of rooftops, yards and roads and will eventually end up in streams, rivers, lakes, canals, and oceans.
