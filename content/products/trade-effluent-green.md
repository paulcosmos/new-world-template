## Trade Effluent (TE) Drain Tags (Green)

Trade effluent is any liquid that's discharged into the sewer from a business, industrial or trade process. These include food and drink production, chemical manufacture, engineering, swimming pools and vehicle washing facilities. This can be treated by an on-site effluent treatment facility. Unless a consent to discharge permit has been approved by the local water authority to go to the foul sewer.
