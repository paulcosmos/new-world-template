We have used Steve now on four separate jobs:- the outside of the house, the hall, stairs and landing, the sitting room and, most recently, the kitchen and family room. He has always done a very good job at a very reasonable price. He takes care over what he’s doing, and is very thorough. We have recommended him to family and friends and they have all been more than satisfied with his work and with the price charged. We would not hesitate to recommend him.
