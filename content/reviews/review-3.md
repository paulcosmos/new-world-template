Simon acted as lead contractor for refitting our bathroom and cloakroom, using fittings we sourced and to our design. He did an excellent job, managing other contractors (electrics, joinery, plastering, tiling) as needed. His workmanship is excellent as you'd expect, but he's also very neat and tidy, communicates well, is easy to get along with, has a good eye for detail and making things look right, and when the inevitable problems occur, he handles everything calmly with a 'can do' attitude. In short, he's an absolutely first-rate tradesman and we'd highly recommend him to anyone.
