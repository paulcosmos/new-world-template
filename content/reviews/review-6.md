Florin is an exceptional worker and so are the people he employs. They worked right through one the hottest day of the summer building three walls. They look just as we wanted, nothing was to much trouble. When they had finished the building the garden was put back and apart from the new walls you would not know they had been there. I would recommend him for any building work but not until he has come back to do some more work for us. Many thanks Florin and team.
