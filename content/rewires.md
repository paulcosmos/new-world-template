Electricity is transmitted around each property through electrical circuits. Each circuit is coated in resilient electrical wiring to ensure electricity runs from the mains supply to the electrical appliances and features within the property, safely. Unfortunately, over time, this wiring is susceptible to wear and tear, which if left unresolved will deteriorate further, leading to major issues such as overheating, appliance damage and in the worst case, electrical fires. 

At {{<company>}}, we advise all property owners to undergo a full rewiring service every 25 years, however, there are various signs to look out for that could suggest your wiring needs to be replaced sooner. These signs include:

- Visibly frayed wiring 
- Cracked wires 
- Burning smell in the property 
- Smoking outlets 
- Frequent circuit tripping 

If experiencing any of the signs above, or any additional issues are detected, we strongly emphasise the importance of seeking assistance from a professional team like ourselves.
