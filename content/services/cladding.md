## Cladding

Choosing cladding as a surface facade on your future or current projects can not only have a high impact on the visual appeal of the building, it also works safeguards, protects and provides an additional layer of thermal value to your buildings elevated areas. We can supply and fit cladding in a range of styles and materials, including:

- Timber cladding (Cedar, Larch, Thermo clad, Sheet material).
- Metal cladding (Galvanised, steel, aluminium).
- Plastic composite or cement board cladding.
- Brick cladding or imitation stone.
