# Gas Compliance 

We can ensure your gas central heating system Carbon monoxide is a highly dangerous gas which can cause serious illness and death that you can't see, smell or taste. We can install CO detectors and alarms in your home that will quickly alert you of any dangerous gases that may be escaping.

* Protects you and your family from life-threatening gases
* Recommended to check CO detectors when you check smoke alarms
