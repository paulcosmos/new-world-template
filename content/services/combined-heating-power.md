Combined Heat and Power (CHP) is an efficient process that captures and utilises heat that is formed by the electricity generation process. CHP systems run by heat and electrical power simultaneously being generated, using an engine that runs on a single fuel. This fuel can be anything from a fossil fuel to a renewable fuel and can be selected based on personal preference. With this, using a combination of heat and power, alongside the natural gas alternative that is typically used in CHP systems, this is a great way of decreasing fuel usage and reducing monthly costs.
