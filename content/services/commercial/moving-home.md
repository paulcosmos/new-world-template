## Emergency Call Outs

I operate a **24 hour-7 day** emergency response service.

Usually locksmiths are called as a result of negative circumstances. i.e. you are locked out or have broken or lost your keys etc.

I am aware of this and do my best to minimise the stress of these events. I endeavour to gain entry using a policy known within the locksmith industry as **non-destructive opening**, only using force and destructive options as an **absolute last resort**.

As I don't operate a call out charge you will only be charged for the locksmiths time from the time he arrives at your premises.
