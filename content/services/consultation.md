## Roof Consultations

We offer consultations to advise customers on the benefits and drawbacks of each roof style and materials to help them select the best choice for their property.

For example lead roofs are long lasting and relatively easy to repair, where as flat roofs are less expensive than lead roofs, beneficial for garage roofs but require more maintenance. Slate roofs are very durable and attractive, but they can be quite heavy and require special installation techniques.
