## Domestic Cleaning

We offer two types of domestic services. The first is a general weekly or bi-weekly maintenance clean of the property which consists of hoovering, mopping floors, bathroom cleaning, kitchen cleaning and the basics to keep a house clean and tidy. The second service is a one-off deep clean, which consists of a more thorough cleaning of the house.

* Weekly or bi-weekly cleaning available
* Optional additional cleaning services
