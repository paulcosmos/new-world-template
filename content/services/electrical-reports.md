Landlords and business owners are deemed responsible for the electrical safety of their properties and must therefore undergo routine safety checks by a competent team of electrical experts. 

As a mandatory requirement, we strongly emphasise the importance of undergoing an EICR service every 5 years to ensure all certificates are kept up to date and to eliminate potentially hazardous and disruptive issues from arising. 

At {{<company>}}, we cover all bases with our EICR safety checks, inspecting and testing a comprehensive range of electrical aspects throughout the property/premises to ensure all aspects comply with current safety standards. 

Alongside ensuring the property is electrically safe, EICR services also come with a range of additional benefits, including:

* Preservation of electrical aspects 
* Cost-effective longterm 
* Insurance benefits 
* Identifies damage to sockets and switches 
* Ensures the property complies with relevant regulations

At the end of each EICR service, we issue a detailed report explaining our findings, including advised reparation work (relative to the test and inspection results). Once all work is completed, we provide the landlord or business owner with a renewed certificate deeming the property electrically safe for the next 5 years.
