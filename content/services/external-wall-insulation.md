## External Wall Insulation

External wall insulation is an energy efficient means of upgrading your property's thermal performance. The system involves encasing the property exterior with insulation boards. This is followed with mesh reinforcement, a base coat and a final decorative finish. 

* Reduce heat loss and save on your heating bills. 
* Ideal for 'hard to heat' homes
