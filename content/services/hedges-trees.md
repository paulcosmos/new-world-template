## Hedge & Tree Care

We provide a select number of arborist services, including trimming and pruning hedges and trees, stump removal and tree felling. We understand that your trees are valuable investments and we use only the most effective practices to care for them.

All garden waste is recycled or disposed of in an ethical, legal and environmentally friendly manner.
