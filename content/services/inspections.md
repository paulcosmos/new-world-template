### PAT Testing & Certification

Our PAT tests are comprised of a full testing process on all portable appliances throughout the property. At {{<company>}} we recommend PAT testing on an annual basis to upkeep the condition of all portable appliances, preventing costly damages that could be potentially unsafe if left unattended to over time. 

### EICR Reports 

Our EICR reports include an array of crucial tests such as wiring tests, accessory tests, consumer unit inspections and so much more. Whilst it is a mandatory requirement for all landlords to undergo an EICR inspection every 5 years or change of tenancy, at {{<company>}}, we also advise all domestic property owners to undergo their EICR safety checks every 5 years and commercial businesses every 3 years. This ensures all electrical aspects are in an optimal condition, keeping the property environment and electrics secure at all times. 

### Fire Alarm Testing

Our fire alarm tests are comprised of a comprehensive check-up and inspection of the entire system to ensure the fire alarm is performing at its highest, safest potential. At {{<company>}}, we issue our fire alarm testing certificates to commercial businesses at the end of each test. These certificates are valid for 1 year, however, we emphasise the importance of undergoing regular weekly testing to ensure there are no failures within the system. 

### Emergency Light Testing

We cover every base with our light fixture tests, working with a diverse range of light fixture types and styles to ensure we are able to assist and accommodate both domestic and commercial customers. Our light fixture tests are comprised of a full inspection and testing of the light function to ensure all aspects are maintained in an optimal condition. This is crucial as it allows us to detect any issues prematurely, preventing major damages from arising that could put the property in a potentially hazardous position.
