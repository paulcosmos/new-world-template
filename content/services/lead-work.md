Our lead flashing solution is perfect for homeowners who are looking to safeguard their roof from moisture penetration at joints such as the chimney, vent pipes, walls and window openings. 

Lead flashing is comprised of multiple lead sheets positioned in a unique way to specifically reduce the chance of water ingress. This, therefore, preserves the property and its structure by eliminating the chance of major issues such as damp, mould and mildew build-up, and so much more.
