## Water Mist Fire Suppression Systems

Water mist fire suppression systems utilise high pressure and specialized sprinkler heads to create fine water droplets that act as a mist, drastically increasing the overall suppression surface area whilst using much less water than a standard sprinkler system.

* Immediate and highly effective suppression activation. 
* Less water results in less water damage overall
