## Pooch Pedicures

Keeping your dog’s claws trimmed is essential for maintaining their health. Long claws can cause cause tender and sore paws, making it hard for your dog to walk and can lead to long-lasting posture problems later in life. Dog claws should be kept short so the claws don't hit the ground when walking. Vets recommend trimming your dogs claws every few weeks for optimal health.

If you're not comfortable trimming your dogs claws yourself, you can bring them to us. Our professional grooming team are well versed in painlessly trimming claws on all breeds of dog, ensuring your pooch is able to run, walk and play just fine.
