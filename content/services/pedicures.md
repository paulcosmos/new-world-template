## Pooch Pedicures

Keeping your dog’s nails trimmed is essential for maintaining their health. Long nails can cause cause tender and sore paws, making it hard for your dog to walk and can lead to long-lasting posture problems later in life. Dog nails should be kept short so they don't hit the ground when walking. Vets recommend trimming every few weeks for optimal health.

If you're not comfortable trimming your dogs nails yourself, we can do it for you. Our team are experienced in all breeds. Just pop in and see us. There's no need to book an appointment, but we recommend giving us a quick call beforehand to let us know you're coming.
