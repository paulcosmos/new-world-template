## Power Flushing

Power flushing removes grime and sludge that can build up in your central heating system over time. Flushing the system helps to prolong the life of boilers and piping, and ensures your central heating system is operating to its full potential

* Helps to prevent breakdowns and failures with your boiler
* Recommended to flush your system regularly.
