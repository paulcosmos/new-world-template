## Power Flushing

Is your central heating system not working as well as it should? Contact us for a comprehensive power flushing service. Power flushing removes grime and sludge that can build up in your central heating system over time, helping to prolong the life of boilers and ensures your central heating system is operating to its full potential.

* Helps to prevent breakdowns and failures with your boiler
* Recommended to flush your system regularly.
