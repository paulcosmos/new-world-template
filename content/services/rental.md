## Rental Income & CGT

Due to Budget changes made in 2017 our clients have been mainly faced with the following potential problems: 

- Reduction of tax deductible interest on rental properties 
- Abolishing of Tear and Wear Allowances
- £50,000 Child Benefit threshold and higher rate taxpayers
- Reclaim tax deductible brokers fees/mortgage product fees
- Property upgrade explanations
- Division of the rental profits as per Mortgage Deed
- Allocation of the rental profits in unequal shares
