## Emergency Door Entry

I provide a **24 hour** 7 days a week, 365 days a year emergency service in the event that you are locked out of your home.

Where possible I operate a policy, known within the locksmiths industry as non-destructive opening, which means I will always attempt to use traditional lock picking or other industry recognised entry methods to gain entry.

I will only drill locks or use other methods where it has become impossible or impractical to gain entry by using non-destructive techniques.
