## Faulty Lock Replacement

Why wait until the lock fails completely, before you have it changed. The chances are you will be on the outside trying to get in when it happens. I carry all types of rim cylinder locks (better known as Yale locks), mortice locks (BS 3621) compliant, as well as numerous other locking mechanisms. I can usually replace 99% of locks in a single visit.
