## UPVC Door Locks

Due to the variety of UPVC door locks on the market it is extremely unlikely that any locksmith would have a suitable replacement on his vehicle. When these locks fail I will need to make an inspection to see if firstly the lock can be repaired and secondly, if it can't be repaired determine the type of lock needed for replacement.

This will necessitate a second visit, normally within 24 to 48 hours.
