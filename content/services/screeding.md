## Screeding


Liquid screeds are a superior choice for your floor elevation frequently being used in conjunction with underfloor heating. Providing higher thermal heat transfer and retention of heat for longer periods as underfloor heating pipes allow more surface contact area with floor screed saving cost on energy bills.

Liquid screeds reduce curing times, helping speed along any project that will allow for early foot traffic and sooner floor coverings.
