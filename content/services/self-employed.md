## Self Employment & Freelance

We will complete your tax return, calculate your tax liability, file the return on-line and liaise with the client on the amounts to be paid and when they are due. This also includes:

- SA100/SA103/SA302 and Tax Overview Forms
- SA800 Partnership Tax Return
- Self Assessment Appeals/Compliance Procedure
- £50,000 Child Benefit Income Tax Disclosure
- Claim for Tax relief for expenses of employment (P87)
- Unique Tax Reference Request and Confirmation
- State Pension Request and BR19 application form
- Income Tax Calculations for companies directors
- Work from home allowance
- IR35 HM Revenue & Customs Compliance
- Tax Return Aspect and Full Inquiries Assistance
- Full bookkeeping , application of HMRC accredited Apps
