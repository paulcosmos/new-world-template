## Silicone Rendering

Silicone rendering adds an extra protective barrier to the external walls of your property and is available in a range of colours and finishes to suit your style. Silicone is a rubbery polymer, making it particularly resistant to water and cracking.

* Wide range of colours and finishes available 
* Durable and weather reistant
