## Full Washes & Styling

Our expert, highly experienced dog grooming team are on hand to get even the scruffiest of pups looking prim and proper again. Our pampering services are tailored to each dog, their breed, and coat type, leaving them feeling their best and smelling even better. 

We stock a range of products, including deep cleansing shampoo for dirty dog, whitening shampoo (it's like Daz for dogs!) and hypoallergenic for dogs with sensitive skin.

You can be sure your pet will get the absolute best treatment from our friendly, dog-loving team, and for those dogs that are a little bit nervous and need extra time and attention we can cater a groom to suit their needs.
