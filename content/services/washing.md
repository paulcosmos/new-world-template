## Full Washes & Styling

Our expert, highly experienced dog grooming team are on hand to get even the most dishevelled of canines looking prim and proper again. Our styling services include a shampoo wash, dry, and an all over trimming of the fur, being sure to remove any knots or tangles in the process. 

You can be sure your pet will get the absolute best treatment from our friendly, dog-loving team, and for those dogs that don't like being away from their owner, we're happy for you to sit in with us and keep them company.
