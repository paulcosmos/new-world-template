## Sustainability & Caring for Our Planet
Our Vision has evolved into a clear and unifying purpose which reflects our commitment to sustainability and the three ‘pillars’ of environmental, social and governance matters.

We aim to minimise our impact on the environment and maximise the implementation of initiatives wherever possible by creating sustainable, high quality and environmentally friendly communities and developments.

We would create an energy strategy division monitor our heat and hot water, solar system efficiency to residents, with major system efficiencies reducing C02 and energy wastage. The development-wide rainwater harvesting and sustainable urban drainage system is currently the largest in the UK and recycles rainwater through natural drainage features before filtering it and returning it to our new homes for use in washing machines and flushing toilets.
