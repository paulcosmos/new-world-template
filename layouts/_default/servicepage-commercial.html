Commercial & Industrial Services

Electrical Services for Businesses

We provide a wide range of electrical services to commercial customers, including 3-phase electrical installations for a range of applications such as cafes, restaurants, shops, offices, agricultural and industrial businesses, and more.

Commercial Electrical Systems

Our expert electrical engineers are on hand to provide LED lighting and upgrades from the smallest office or cafe to the largest warehouse or superstore. We can also provide telephone, power sockets and data cabling throughout your workplace.

  • CAT6 Data Connections
  • Office Dado Rails
  • Bars / Restaurants / Kitchens
  • Shop-floors & Feature Lighting

Industrial Installations

Whether it's food and beverage plants or manufacturing facilities, our fully CHAS accredited electricians can provide a wide range of industrial electrical installations across a broad spectrum of industries.

  • 3 Phase Installations
  • Metal Conduit Systems
  • Cable Mangement Trays
  • Cable Jointing

EICR Testing & Inspection

Ensure your business is safe with our complete electrical safety inspections. We also offer portable appliance testing (PAT) and EICR certificates. It is recommended to get your property and appliances tested at least every:

  • Commercial - 5 years
  • Industrial - 3 years
