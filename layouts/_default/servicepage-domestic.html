New Build Wiring

All our engineers are qualified to carry out new-build wiring that complies with British standard BS7671:2018 (IET Wiring Regulations). We prefer to be involved from the electrical design stage and break all new build wiring projects into smaller stages:

  • Electrical System Design
  • First-fix Wiring Installations
  • Second-fix Fixtures & Fittings
  • Testing & Commissioning

Smart Home Automation

  • Home Security Control

  • Lighting Control
  • Future-proof Adaptable

EICR Testing & Inspection

Ensure your home or business is safe with our complete electrical safety inspections. We offer portable appliance testing (PAT) and domestic/landlord EICR certificates. It is recommended to get your property and appliances tested at least every:

  • Private Domestic - 10 years
  • Rented Domestic - 5 years
  • Commercial - 5 years
  • Industrial - 3 years
