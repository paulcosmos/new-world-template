Solar Panels (PV)

Solar energy is a 100% clean, renewable power source. Solar Photovoltaic (PV) Panels utilise the latest technology to convert the sun's rays into to usable electricity, providing a high power output even on a cloudy day. The modular design of solar panels allows for multiple layout configurations on almost any roof type.

  • 5 Year Guarantee
  • 1kWh – 15kWh Systems
  • Built-in or Retrofit
  • Reduced Energy Bills

Solar Battery Storage

Solar Batteries allow you to store excess solar electricity generated from your solar panels, giving you a surplus of energy throughout the day that can be used at night. This reduces the amount of grid electricity you need to buy, helping you to cut-down on electricty bill long term.

  • 10 Year Guarantee
  • Expandable Systems
  • App enabled control
  • Reduced Energy Bills
